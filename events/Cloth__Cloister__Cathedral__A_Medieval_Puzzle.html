"Cloth, Cloister, Cathedral: A Medieval Puzzle" Anne McCants Professor of History and MacVicar Faculty Fellow Massachusetts Institute of technology Wednesday, September 28, 5 PM East Duke 204D East Campus, Duke University Sponsored by the Dept. of Art, Art History & Visual Studies and the Center for Medieval and Renaissance Studies Prof. McCants studies the economic aspects of church building in the High Middle Ages. She is especially interested in the financial arrangements that allowed for the construction of Gothic cathedrals as well as the economic impact of those building projects.
