Durham, NC: Introducing Students to the Community in which We Live, Learn, and Serve

Start Time: 15:15

End Time: 16:30

What role did Duke¿s Allen Building play in the Civil Rights History of Durham? What can we learn about Durham from 2010 Census data? In what ways do Duke¿s service-learning courses participate in Durham¿s Downtown Revitalization Projects? These are only a few of the questions faculty might consider when orienting students to Durham. At this hands-on and interactive workshop, participants will learn about actual and virtual resources on Durham through a Durham Library Guide; be connected to their subject librarian for further development of course-specific resources; and share ideas about ways to orient students to our community.
