Duke University Dance Program and Art of The Moving Image Present The Premier Evening of: Dance for the Camera Out Doors.* Come see an eclectic array of Dance Films from New York City¿s Dance Films Association* as well as Duke student works created in Dance for the Camera Spring 2011 (DS106s). Program Includes: "Flying Lesson" (USA), "Ebony Goddess" (Bahia, Brazil), "The Mysteries of Nature" (South Korea), "The Cost of Living" (UK), and student works: by: Danika Manso-Brown, Sarah McCaffrey and Lila Thornton. FREE and open to the public. Bring your own blanket! Free Popcorn!!! (Rain location: The Ark Dance Studio, behind the East Campus Student Union). *Dance Films Association's annual Dance on Camera Festival is co-sponsored by the Film Society of Lincoln Center and supported by the National Endowment for the Arts.
