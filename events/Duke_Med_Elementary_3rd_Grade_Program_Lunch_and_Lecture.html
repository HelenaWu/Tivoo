Duke Med Elementary 3rd Grade Program Lunch and Lecture

Start Time: 12:00

End Time: 13:00

Duke Med Elementary, affiliated with GPSC and originating from Duke Medical School, is a dynamic service club that aims to teach local 3rd grade students the importance of healthy lifestyles. We will cover curriculum on the cardiovascular system (with a pig heart dissection!) in the fall. This is a one hour lunch with a local elementary school in Durham. Through this program, we hope to spark interest in the health sciences and encourage these young-ens to pursue higher levels of education. Activities will include lunch, lecture, and small group activities.
